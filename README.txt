To play the game:
  - The whole project folder must be downloaded, included with all the following folders provided:
      .vscode
      Assets
      MonoBleedingEdge
      Packages
      ProjectSettings
      S22_Proj_2A_Castro_Carlysse
      .gitignore
  - Open "S22_Proj_2A_Castro_Carlysse"
  - Game should be launched!

How to play: 
  - Collect the bread on the screen
  - Get across the lanes towards the top of the screen
  - Touch the top of the screen to secure your point
  - Score the indicated amount of points to win!
